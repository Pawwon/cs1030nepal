Festivals

Nepal has many kinds of people, languages, and traditions. Festivals such as Dashain and Tihar are celebrated with family and friends. Religion is important, and you can see temples, monasteries, and prayer flags throughout the country. People in Nepal are friendly and welcoming. Visiting Nepal helps people learn about its culture and traditions.

Major Festivals

  • Dashain - celebrated in September/October, symbolizing the victory of good over evil.
  • Tihar - Festival of Lights, celebrated in October/November.
  • Teej - women's fasting festival, celebrated in August/September.

Traditions

Nepalese culture includes traditional clothing, music, and dances, passed down through generations.
