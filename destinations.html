Destinations

Nepal has many places for visitors to explore. People can go trekking in the mountains, visit lakes in Pokhara, or see animals in national parks like Chitwan. There are also temples, old cities, and beautiful viewpoints. Nepal offers something for everyone, from adventure to calm and peaceful places.

Pokhara

Pokhara is famous for its lakes, paragliding, and stunning views of the Annapurna range. Phewa Lake is one of the most popular spots where visitors can enjoy boating.

Chitwan

Chitwan National Park is home to rhinos, tigers, elephants, and many bird species. A jungle safari here gives visitors a chance to see wildlife up close.
