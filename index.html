Discover Nepal

Nepal is a small nation located between India and China. It is famous for its natural beauty, rivers, forests, and the Himalayas. Nepal also has a rich history and culture. This website provides information about Nepal's land, people, culture, and popular places to visit.

About Nepal

Nepal is home to eight of the world's fourteen highest peaks, including Mount Everest (Sagarmatha). It is a multiethnic, multilingual, and multicultural country where Hinduism and Buddhism thrive together. Kathmandu, the capital city, is known as the "City of Temples."

Fun Facts

  • Only country in the world with a non-rectangular national flag.
  • Birthplace of Lord Buddha (Lumbini).
  • Home to Mount Everest, the highest peak in the world.
  • Over 120 languages are spoken across the country.
