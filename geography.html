Mountains

Nepal is famous for its mountains, including Mount Everest, the highest peak in the world. The country has many tall mountains, hills, and valleys. These mountains attract hikers, climbers, and nature lovers from around the world. The landscapes are full of forests, rivers, and wildlife. Nepal's mountains make it a beautiful and exciting place to explore.
